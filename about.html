About Me

R Jarrett Dowd

R Jarrett Dowd is an Author and Web-Developer from Cleveland Ohio. Born in Jamestown New York R moved to Cleveland at the young age of 5. As a teenager R became interested in web development by creating a simple landing page for his friends podcast. At the age of twenty three R wrote his first novel Phases Of Reason I: The Eight Ball. He now continues to work on his second novel while working on various web development projects.
